Hello, chef!

You are a fast-food restaurant chef. Your task is to prepare orders as it's written on the receipts.

Rules

  • You prepare orders one by one
  • To add an ingredient, use keyboard keys
  • Letters are reserved for ingredients, digits are for beverages (1-4) and sides (9-0)
  • Every 3 seconds you loose 1 point — chop chop
  • Mistakes cost you points as listed below
  • Mistakes cannot be undone — go for next ingredient
  • Deliver the order with [Enter]
  • Remember to always start and end a burger with a bun!
  • You have 3 minutes, get the biggest score you can!
